Select your very own barrel of Jack

CHOOSING YOUR
BARREL
Come pay us a visit at the Jack Daniel Distillery for a
one-of-a-kind whiskey experience. Our Distilling
Team will personally guide you through a tasting to
find the barrel that’s right for you. If you can’t visit,
we’ll send you whiskey samples from three
different barrels, each with their own distinct flavor
profile. Or, we can simply have our Distilling
Team make a barrel selection based on your
preferences.
COMMEMORATING THE EXPERIENCE
A series of accompanying premium keepsake
elements will help authenticate your experience.
All of your selection's unique details, from origin to
bottling date, will be included within this
experience. A special plaque in the Jack Daniel's
Personal Selection Room at our distillery will also
commemorate your selection.
SAVORING YOUR
WHISKEY
The entirety of your barrel’s whiskey will arrive in
individual Single Barrel Select bottles, adorned with
a personalized medallion. Each gift box also comes
with an exclusive Personal Collection sticker -
featuring Master Distiller, Chris Fletcher’s
signature. These collectibles are perfect as gifts for a
group, or ideal for an individual to savor.
