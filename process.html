04 / CHARCOAL MELLOWING

MELLOWED DROP BY
DROP

We let our whiskey slowly drip through 10 ft of hard
sugar maple charcoal so it comes out with its
distinctive smoothness. It’s a costly, time
consuming process and if it sounds extravagant,
well, it is.

story of our charcoal mellowing
05 / BARREL MAKING

THE INGREDIENT WE
MAKE OURSELVES

A barrel is more than a container for whiskey. It’s an
ingredient. That’s why we proudly age in new,
handcrafted white oak barrels, the same way we’ve
been doing for more than 150 years.

story of our barrels
06 / MATURATION

HURRYING NEVER
HELPED GOOD WHISKEY

Our whiskey goes into the barrel colorless and raw.
Years later, it will pour out a beautiful amber, its
rawness turned smooth and complex. Exactly how
many years later? That’s a question decided by
Mother Nature and Father Time.
